What Could Go Wrong Even If The Catchment Looks Good

Arrival risk

Recent Pune reporting still points to traffic disruption in the corridor, while PMRDA's Metro Line 3 page says the line is meant to solve long-running congestion and is still in phased delivery. Access is improving, but it is not solved today.

Weather and road-condition risk

Rain-related road stress reporting is a reminder that the commute experience can worsen sharply in bad weather. Outdoor sport can survive that, but only if circulation and site upkeep stay clean.

Differentiation risk

Nearby supply already talks publicly about parking, lounges, cafe service, changing rooms, indoor play, and even glow formats. In a cluster like this, success attracts more supply, so the location alone will not carry weak execution.

What Must Be Physically Checked Before Shortlisting

Gate and access friction

Time the trip from the main road turn-in to the actual parking spot during office rush, not only on a calm afternoon.

Parking truth

Count real usable cars and two-wheelers when all courts are live, not the theoretical number shown on a brochure.

Rain behavior

Check puddling, mud transfer, clean circulation, and whether players can still arrive and leave without the experience feeling messy.

Operator-versus-location edge

Confirm how much of the current success belongs to the location itself versus the present operator's hospitality, community building, and upkeep.
